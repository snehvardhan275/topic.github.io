Disaster Management and Recovery Platform

Used SDG:

SDG Number: 4 - Disaster Response Coordinator

Explanation:
  • Problem: Post-disaster recovery is often hindered by poor resource allocation and lack of coordination between organizations.
  • Solution: A centralized dashboard for disaster coordinators that:
    • Tracks resources (food, water, medical supplies) and allocates them efficiently.
    • Monitors affected areas using satellite data and real-time updates.
    • Coordinates efforts between government agencies, NGOs, and volunteers.
  • Impact:
    • Social Impact: Accelerates recovery and reduces human suffering.
    • Stakeholders: Governments, international relief organizations, and donors.
    • Revenue Model: Funded by government disaster management budgets and international grants.
