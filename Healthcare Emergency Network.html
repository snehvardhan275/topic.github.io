Paramedics and Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs) Platform

Used SDG:

SDG Number: 2 - Paramedics and Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs)

Explanation:
  • Problem: Paramedics face challenges in accessing patient records and finding the nearest available healthcare facilities.
  • Solution: A cloud-based network that:
    • Provides instant access to patient medical history through secure databases.
    • Suggests the nearest healthcare facility with real-time capacity updates.
    • Includes GPS navigation for the fastest routes.
  • Impact:
    • Efficiency: Enables paramedics to make informed decisions during emergencies.
    • Stakeholders: Hospitals, ambulance services, and patients.
    • Revenue Model: Licensing fees for hospitals and government funding for public healthcare integration.
